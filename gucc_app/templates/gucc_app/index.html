Welcome to GUCC

Welcome to the Glasgow Uni Canoe Club!

We are an inclusive kayaking club for all skill levels!

We meet weekly to improve our kayaking skills, but more importantly to have fun

We have a session in the uni pool every thursday followed up by FREE PIZZA and good vibes in bank street kitchen

We also organise kayak trips and events across scotland to rivers, events and more!

Come join us if you are a kayaking pro sending grade 5s or if you have never been in a kayak before (I hadn't!)
